What is Ultimate Frisbee?

Ultimate frisbee is a high pace game played on a football field. 14 people play at a time with 7 players on each team.
To win your team needs to get to 15 points or end the game with the most points after an hour and a half. To score
a players needs advance the disc down the field by throwing the disc to their players until they catch the disc in the endzone

Some Important Rules to Remember

  • You cannot run with the disc you have to establish a pivot foot
  • You have 10 seconds to throw the disc which is called the stall
  • Ultimate Frisbee is a non contact sport
  • It is a self officated sport which means there are no referees
  • You call your own fouls
